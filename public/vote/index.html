    WAVES
    vote

    • Every 100,000 blocks nodes votes for monetary policy (decrease or increase miner block reward by 0,5 WAVES). Participants of our pool can decide how we will vote.

      We are distributing Dodllnode token with every payout in equal quantaty with Waves reward.

      More Dodllnode tokens on address - greater power of the voice.

      You need to login with Keeper and had Dodllnode token for voting on this page.

    • Choose how much WAVES (in your own opinion) must be at each block as miners reward.

      If your option will be more than current - your vote will be counting for increase reward.

      If your option will be less than current - your vote will be counting for decrease reward.

      If your option will be equal to current - your vote will be counting as not to change reward.

      Example: you are choose 6.5 WAVES as reward for block (it counted as increase) and >50% of Waves nodes voted for increase. So when reward will be already 6.5 WAVES for next voting your choise will be counting as not to change reward.

      In this way you can vote one time for all voting periods and also revote anytime you want.

    • For voting operation you need to pay 0.009 Dodllnode and 0.009 WAVES comission.

      All tokens (except comission) remains on your wallet.

      You can vote once for all voting periods and revote anytime you want.

    • You can vote from any Waves address (not nessesary from one that leased to Dodllnode)

      If you voted you can sell or transfer your Dodllnode tokens. After you voted you can sell or transfer your Dodllnode tokens. But in this case, your vote will not counted anymore.

    • Absolutely. When you voting you just write on blockchain information about your address choise. And we counting it for every voting period.

      If you don't want to vote from your leasing wallet (for any reasons), you are always can transfer Dodllnode tokens to other wallet and voting from it.
